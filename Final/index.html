About our Organization

Society Against Misinformation (SAM) is a non-profit organization dedicated to the analysis and prevention of misinformation on the internet. We strive to inform the users of the internet about the dangers of misinformation and present ideas to make a better future for the web. It's important, then, to first define misinformation and a common sister term:

Misinformation Disinformation
Incorrect or Misleading Information False information deliberately and often covertly spread (as by the planting of rumors) in order to influence public opinion or obscure the truth
Source: Merriam-Webster

The defining difference that is critical is the intent of how information is shared. Information shared with the intent to influence is disinformation, and information shared that is misleading is misinformation. Another interesting aspect is people who unknowingly share disinformation are propagating misinformation. Throughout this website we present why misinformation is a big concern for our society, social media's role, and some possible solutions for stopping misinformation from being shared.
